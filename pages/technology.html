Estate planning, simplified.

Total estate value, nil-rate bands, gifts in the seven-year window, projected inheritance tax — all calculated automatically and kept up to date as your circumstances change.

Your will, lasting powers of attorney, and trust documents are tracked with status alerts. If your will is five years old, the system flags it. If an LPA needs renewing, you'll know.

Built, not bought

Built for our clients. By our team.

Every line of code behind Family Capital was written in-house by our own development team. We don't license our technology — it's built around what our clients actually need, and it evolves with them.

When we see a better way to do something, we build it. When a client suggests an improvement, it happens. No waiting for a vendor's roadmap, no compromising on features because a third-party platform doesn't support them.

Over twenty years of building technology for financial services has taught us that the best client experience comes from controlling every part of it yourself.

It also means we control our own security. Our systems are reviewed and stress-tested by our in-house cyber security team, encrypted end to end, and accessible only to our clients and our advisers. There's no shared infrastructure, no multi-tenancy — just our platform, our people, and the families we look after.
