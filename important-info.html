Regulatory disclosures and risk warnings

Regulatory Status

Risk Warnings

The value of investments and the income from them can go down as well as up, and you may not get back the amount originally invested. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results.

Investments in equities, bonds, property, and other asset classes carry varying degrees of risk. There is no guarantee that any investment strategy will achieve its objectives. You should not invest in anything unless you understand the nature of the investment and the risks involved.

Some investments may not be readily realisable, and it may be difficult to sell or realise them. Obtaining reliable information about their value or the extent of the risks to which they are exposed may also be difficult.

Currency Risk

Where investments are denominated in currencies other than sterling, changes in exchange rates may cause the value of those investments to decrease or increase.

Tax Treatment

The tax treatment of investments depends on the individual circumstances of each client and may be subject to change in the future. Tax reliefs referred to are those currently available and their value depends on the circumstances of the individual investor. Levels and bases of taxation may change.

We recommend that you seek specialist tax advice where appropriate.

Financial Services Compensation Scheme

Family Capital Limited is covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS). You may be entitled to compensation from the scheme if we cannot meet our obligations. This depends on the type of business and the circumstances of the claim. For investment claims, the FSCS covers up to £85,000 per person per firm. Higher limits (currently £120,000) apply to cash deposits held with banks. Further information about compensation scheme arrangements is available from the FSCS at www.fscs.org.uk.
